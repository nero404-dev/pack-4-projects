About

The Santos de Cartier collection is a timeless symbol of innovation and elegance, originally created in 1904 by Louis Cartier for the Brazilian aviator Alberto Santos-Dumont. Designed as one of the first wristwatches in history, it addressed the need for practicality and ease of use during flight. The collection is instantly recognizable by its square case, visible screws, and sleek yet bold design, which reflect both functionality and sophistication. Over the decades, the Santos de Cartier has evolved, incorporating modern features like automatic movements and interchangeable straps, while preserving its heritage and iconic aesthetic. It is a true testament to Cartier's ability to blend tradition with innovation.

Its designs celebrate the spirit of aviation and the pioneering vision of Alberto Santos-Dumont, making it a favorite for those who appreciate fine craftsmanship and history. Available in a variety of sizes, materials, and finishes, the collection offers something for every style, from classic gold to contemporary steel and bi-metal options. Whether worn casually or formally, the Santos de Cartier exudes a sense of sophistication and confidence, making it an enduring icon in the world of haute horology.
